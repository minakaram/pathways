Job Description

We are seeking a passionate and experienced Business Ethics and Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) Tutor to join our team of dedicated educators. As a Business Ethics and CSR Tutor, you will play a crucial role in shaping the minds of future business leaders, imparting knowledge and fostering responsible business practices.

Job Responsibilities

  • Develop and deliver engaging and interactive lectures, workshops, and seminars on a variety of business ethics and CSR topics, including ethical decision-making, corporate governance, sustainability, and social impact.
  • Design and implement effective teaching methodologies to cater to diverse learning styles and ensure that all students grasp the core concepts of business ethics and CSR.
  • Assess student progress through examinations, assignments, and case studies to provide constructive feedback and guide their understanding.
  • Stay abreast of current trends and developments in the field of business ethics and CSR to ensure that the curriculum remains relevant and up-to-date.

Minimum Qualifications

  • Master's degree in Business Administration, Business Ethics, Corporate Social Responsibility, or a related field.
  • Minimum of 5 years of experience teaching business ethics and CSR at the undergraduate or postgraduate level.
  • Proven expertise in developing and delivering engaging and effective lectures, workshops, and seminars.
  • Demonstrated ability to assess student progress and provide constructive feedback.
  • Excellent communication, presentation, and interpersonal skills.
  • Passion for business ethics and CSR, and a commitment to promoting responsible business practices.

Benefits

  • Competitive salary and benefits package.
  • Opportunity to make a positive impact on the lives of future business leaders.
  • Collaborative and supportive work environment.
  • Professional development opportunities to enhance your skills and knowledge.
